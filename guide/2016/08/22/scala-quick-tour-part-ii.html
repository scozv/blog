Scala Quick Tour Part II

Aug 22, 2016

Abstract

This is the second part of Scala Quick Tour, in the previous part, we talked about Functional Programming Basis, Functional Type and the Type Inference.

In this article, I will give you a precise introduction of Tail Recursion, which is a very important conception of Functional Programming. After this introduction, I will explain the construction and operations of List[T], using Tail Recursion.

Briefly, the part II will cover:

  • Iteration to Recursion,
  • Recursion vs Tail Recursion,
  • First Glance of Type Parameterization and Pattern Match,
  • Recursion Construction of List[T],
  • head and tail,
  • take and reverse,
  • append and prepend,
  • map and foreach,
  • map and reduce,
  • Read the Source of List.scala.

Currently, This article (Part II) is only written in Chinese.
