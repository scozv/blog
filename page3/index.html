DO NOT Prepare the Development Environment by Click and Click

Abstract

This article will give you an installation.sh script, that can be used to prepare the development environment. Instead of click and click, we just simply run this command in bash：

This article will describe the process of installation.sh, besides, I want to express:

  • Use code, not mouse,
  • Use git, when we use code,
  • Bring the wonderful Development Experience to developers.

The installation.sh will only apply for Ubuntu 14.04+.
